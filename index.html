About me!

Hi, I'm Baran Canakci, a third-year game development student at Grafische Lyceum Utrecht. I chose this field because I enjoy taking a simple idea and logically building it into something bigger. I’m eager to keep learning, especially in C#, and to explore new tools to become a more versatile developer. I’ve worked on group projects and understand Scrum, which helps keep everything on track.
